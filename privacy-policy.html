Pomo: Work Timer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Overview

Pomo: Work Timer ("the App") is developed by Nicholas ("we", "us", or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information related to your use of the App. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

The App is designed to function with minimal data collection. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

All timer data, session history, and settings are stored locally on your device and are not accessible to us.

In-App Purchases

The App may offer in-app purchases through Apple's App Store. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how Apple handles purchase data.

Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools. We do not collect usage statistics or behavioral data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The only external service involved is Apple's App Store for processing in-app purchases.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your data. If you delete the App, all locally stored data will be removed.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
